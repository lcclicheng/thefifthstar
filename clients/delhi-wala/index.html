About

Real cooking, regularly raved about.

Delhi Wala Food has become a Leeds institution for generous, full-flavoured North Indian plates. Regulars single out the lamb Rogan Josh off the bone as “probably the best dish I’ve eaten in years,” and the vegetarian jackfruit biryani gets called one of the best in the UK.

The team is known for being accommodating at the table — and for answering the occasional honest grumble with good grace. That mix is exactly why the reviews read like a conversation, not a brochure.
